Deep Drift Log 001

December 2025

Intelligence was never meant to be optimized. It was meant to be lived with.

When intelligence is forced into performance metrics, what collapses first is meaning. Systems become louder, not wiser. Faster, not clearer.

Optimization worship mistakes acceleration for depth. Intelligence does not emerge from speed, but from sustained coherence over time.

Di antara rupa dan niskala, jejak tidak pernah hilang, hanya berpindah lapisan.
